Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of customer support professionals, ZenDeskPro was born out of a desire to simplify and streamline the support process. Frustrated by the complexity and inefficiency of existing solutions, we set out to create a platform that would make support management intuitive, efficient, and effective.

Over the years, we've grown from a small startup to a leading provider of customer support software, serving thousands of businesses worldwide. Our commitment to innovation, customer-centricity, and continuous improvement has earned us the trust and loyalty of our customers.

Our Mission

At ZenDeskPro, our mission is clear: to empower businesses to deliver outstanding customer support experiences. We believe that exceptional support is the foundation of every successful business, and we're dedicated to providing the tools and resources our customers need to exceed their customers' expectations.

Meet the Team

At ZenDeskPro, we're proud to have a diverse and talented team of professionals who are passionate about customer support. From our product developers and engineers to our customer success managers and support agents, every member of our team is dedicated to helping our customers succeed.
